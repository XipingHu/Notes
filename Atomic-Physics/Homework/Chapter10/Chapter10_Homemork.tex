    \ln \left( \rho_0 / \rho \right) = \dfrac{t}{T} \ln 2 \quad\quad \Rightarrow \quad\quad
    t = T \dfrac{\ln \left( \rho_0 / \rho \right)}{\ln 2} 
  \end{aligned}
\end{equation*}
